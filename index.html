Hello, I'm Christopher Sandor.

I am a creative UX engineer based in North San Diego County with over 20 years of experience blending visual design and accessible front-end development to craft interactive, mobile-responsive websites and web apps.

Skilled in HTML5, CSS3/Sass, JavaScript, and frameworks like AngularJS, Vue.js, Bootstrap and LAMP, I bring designs to life with pixel-perfect precision and performance in mind. I'm also experienced with ServiceNow's Service Portal, WordPress custom theming, and tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Suite. Whether wireframing, prototyping, or coding, I focus on usability, accessibility, and delivering clean, scalable user interfaces.

I am currently a technical consultant at Infocenter, a ServiceNow Elite Partner where I design and develop innovative and custom employee-facing (ITSM, HRSD) and customer-facing (CSM, FSO, HCLS) web applications on ServiceNow's front-end portal framework.
